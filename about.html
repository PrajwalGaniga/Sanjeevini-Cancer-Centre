Meet Your Oncologist

Dr. Rachan Shetty KS is a highly qualified Medical Oncologist with extensive experience in diagnosing and treating different types of cancer. His expertise lies in chemotherapy, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, and personalized cancer treatment plans.

He believes that every patient deserves world-class care with a human touch, and his approach focuses on combining the latest scientific advances with compassionate patient care.

If you are looking for a cancer specialist in Mangalore, Dr. Rachan Shetty KS offers trusted guidance and treatment throughout your cancer journey.
